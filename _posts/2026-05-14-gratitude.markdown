Gratitude!

I'm feeling very overweight. Indeed, since Leo was born I have gained weight at a fast weight than them and Leo is now six.

I also found £1 in a locker at swimming, so that was lovely.  Nova has won some sort of award in school and Leo read to me from a book today in a wonderfully clear way. Kat made me a salad using leaves from our garden. I have arranged to visit a museum with dear friends.
